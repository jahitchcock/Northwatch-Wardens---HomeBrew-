## Special Abilities

### Lay On Hands
**Healing Pool:** 5 HP / Long Rest

You have a pool of healing power that replenishes when you finish a Long Rest. With that pool, you can restore a total of 5 HP.

**Heal (Bonus Action):**
You can touch a creature (which could be yourself) and restore a number of HP to that creature, up to the maximum amount remaining in the pool.

**Purify Poison:**
You can expend 5 HP from the pool of healing to remove the Poisoned condition from the creature; those points don't also restore HP to the creature.
