## The Northwatch Wardens

**Current Status:** Members (after charter signing)

**Reputation Scale:**
- **Recruit (0-2 contracts):** New member, proving themselves
- **Warden (3-5 contracts):** Trusted, full member
- **Veteran (6+ contracts):** Respected, leadership consideration
- **Hero (exceptional service):** Guild legend

**Benefits by Rank:**
- Recruit: Basic pay, basic equipment access
- Warden: Better pay, magic item loans, private quarters
- Veteran: Significant autonomy, mentorship roles, special contracts
- Hero: Guild resources at disposal, voice in decisions

## Tracking Method

**Simple Approach:**
Use Campaign Tracker to note:
- Which factions players interacted with
- Positive/negative interactions
- Favors owed or outstanding
- Current relationship status

**Don't Overcomplicate:** This is background flavor, not core mechanic
