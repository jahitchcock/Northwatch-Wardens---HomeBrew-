# Session Opening Templates

*Ready-to-use templates for starting every session*

\page

## Standard Session Opening

### Template

**Recap (2-3 minutes):**
> "Last session, you [major accomplishment]. You're currently [location/status]. Today, we'll [preview]."

**Set the Scene (2 minutes):**
> Describe current location, time of day, weather, mood

**NPC Interaction (3-5 minutes):**
> Key NPC provides hook, information, or quest

**Player Agency (remainder):**
> "What do you want to do?"

\page

## Opening at Waystone Inn

### Morning Departure

> The common room at Waystone Inn buzzes with early morning activity. Mara serves hot porridge and dark bread while Brenna reviews maps at a corner table. Elric, as always, is buried in a book.

> You've accepted [contract name]. The journey will take [duration]. What do you do before departing?

> You've been gone [duration]. Back at Waystone Inn, [event happening]. Meanwhile, you're [current location/situation].
>
> Let's pick up where we left off.

**Use when:** Continuing mid-adventure

## Opening On the Road

### Standard Travel

> You've been traveling for [duration] through [terrain]. [Weather description]. [Anything unusual].
>
> As you [crest hill/round bend/enter clearing], you see [encounter/destination].

**Use when:** Starting session during travel

\page

### Arrival at Destination

> After [duration] of travel, [destination] comes into view. [Description of location]. [First impression/mood].
>
> What's your approach?

**Use when:** Arriving at adventure location

\page

## Opening Mid-Combat

### Resume Battle

> Let's get initiative back up. Last round, [summary of what happened]. [Current enemy status]. [Current party status].
>
> Top of initiative: [character name], you're up.

**Use when:** Session ended mid-combat (rare, but happens)

\page

## Opening After Time Skip

### Downtime Complete

> [Duration] has passed since you [last adventure conclusion]. During this time:
> - [Player 1] did [downtime activity]
> - [Player 2] did [downtime activity]
> - [etc.]
>
> Today is [date/season]. You're called to the common room. Brenna has news.

**Use when:** Time passes between adventures

\page

## Quick Reference

**Every Session Should Include:**
- Brief recap (2-3 min)
- Current situation (2 min)
- Hook or continuation (3 min)
- Player agency ("What do you do?")

**Total Opening:** 7-10 minutes maximum
