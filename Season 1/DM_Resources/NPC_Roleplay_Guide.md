# NPC Roleplay Guide

*Quick reference for playing the Triad and key NPCs*

\page

## The Triad

### Marshal Brenna Thorne

**Voice:** Firm, military, to the point  
**Body Language:** Stands tall, direct eye contact, crossed arms  
**Speech Pattern:** Short sentences, tactical terms  
**Catchphrase:** "Stay sharp out there."

**Quick Personality:**
- Protective of wardens
- Values competence
- Haunted by past command failures
- Direct but fair

**Use For:** Tactical briefings, field operations, discipline

\page

### Steward Mara Fenwick

**Voice:** Warm, cheerful, slight singsong  
**Body Language:** Busy hands (cooking, organizing), warm smiles  
**Speech Pattern:** Chatty, asks personal questions, gossips  
**Catchphrase:** "Have you eaten, dear?"

**Quick Personality:**
- Motherly and caring
- Knows everyone's business
- Secret intelligence operative
- Shrewd beneath warm exterior

**Use For:** Supplies, gossip, emotional support

\page

### Lorewarden Elric Vael

**Voice:** Distracted, academic, rambling  
**Body Language:** Fidgets, reads while talking, forgets personal space  
**Speech Pattern:** Run-on sentences, excited about facts, trails off  
**Catchphrase:** "Fascinating! Did you know..."

**Quick Personality:**
- Brilliant but scattered
- Obsessed with Aeorian mysteries
- Socially awkward
- Genuinely curious

**Use For:** Research, Echo information, historical context

## Quick NPC Technique

**When improvising NPCs:**

1. **Pick ONE trait** (friendly, suspicious, nervous)
2. **Give them ONE want** (gold, safety, information)
3. **Play that consistently**

**Don't need:**
- Different voice
- Complex backstory
- Deep personality

**Just need:**
- Consistent behavior
- Clear motivation
- Memorable trait

\page

## Roleplaying Tips

**Good Techniques:**
- Vary body language more than voice
- Use catchphrases
- Reference past interactions
- Show, don't tell personality

**Avoid:**
- Forgetting NPC voices mid-scene
- Making every NPC the same
- Over-describing appearance

**Remember:** Personality > Voice Quality
