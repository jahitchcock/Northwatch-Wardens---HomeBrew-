# Wolves of Welton  
*A 2nd–3rd level adventure for 5e*  
*Summary prepared for Northwatch Wardens campaign*

---

## Summary

The village of Welton is under siege by unusually coordinated wolves.  
Flocks vanish overnight, shepherds are attacked, and the village sorcerer has gone missing.  
The council offers a large reward for anyone who can end the threat.

The wolves are not ordinary — they have been magically awakened after an encounter with the missing sorcerer.

---

# Adventure Hooks

- **Passing through:** Welton is a stop on the road; villagers beg for help.  
- **Guild contract:** A posted bounty offers an unusually high reward (800 gp).  
- **Rumors:** Locals speak of wolves opening gates, stealing livestock, and strange magical signs.

# Scene 1 — Wolf Ambush on the Road

**Location:** West of Welton, near a rocky bend and woodland edge.  
**Setup:** Shepherds and sheepdogs are ambushed by a coordinated wolf pack.  
**Encounter:** Wolves attempt to steal sheep, not kill shepherds.  
**Tactics:**  
- Half distract shepherds  
- Half grab sheep and retreat  
- Wolves flee if outmatched  

**Aftermath:**  
- Shepherds share rumors  
- They ask the party to escort them to Welton

---

## Local Rumors (“Shaggy Wolf Stories”)

- Wolves opening locked barns  
- Livestock disappearing without signs of struggle  
- A trader chased off by wolves “appearing from nowhere”  
- A shepherd hearing voices in the woods  
- A failed hunting party ambushed by magical effects  
- A survivor (Featherock) left traumatized

# Scene 2 — Welton

**Description:**  
A farming village on a riverbank, normally peaceful but now strained.  
People are anxious, streets muddy from heavy traffic, and food shortages loom.

**Key Locations:**  
- **Shepherd’s Crook Inn** — central hub, crowded, run by Leanor Slatebeard  
- **Growers’ Hall & Fleecers’ Hall** — local guilds  
- **Featherock’s Room** — traumatized survivor of a wolf attack  

---

## The Shepherd’s Crook

- Packed with shepherds, farmers, and worried locals  
- Leanor Slatebeard welcomes adventurers  
- Featherock is recovering upstairs  
- Featherock reports hearing wolves *talking* during the ambush

---

## The Council Meeting

**Members include:**  
- Tillus Merrion (halfling, Growers & Buyers Association)  
- Father Merriksonn (priest, brother of missing sorcerer)  
- Corel (Fleecers’ Guild)  
- Other human and halfling representatives  

**Key Information:**  
- Wolves are unusually intelligent and coordinated  
- Livestock losses threaten winter survival  
- A previous hunting party was magically ambushed  
- Sorcerer Alexi Merriksonn vanished around the same time

# Scene 3 — Finding the Wolves’ Lair

**Goal:** Track or lure the wolves to locate their den.  
**Options:**  
- Track wolves through the western woods  
- Use Corel’s flock as bait  
- Follow rumors and tracks  

**Skill Checks:**  
- Tracking wolves: Survival DC 14  
- Tracking wolves with prey: Survival DC 11  
- Hiding from wolves: Stealth vs Perception (+3, advantage on scent/sound)  
- Understanding wolf behavior: Nature DC 12  

**Travel:**  
- Woods are dense and slow  
- Crossing takes ~4 hours  

---

# Scene 4 — Optional Encounter: The Injured Owlbear

**Encounter:**  
A wounded, starving owlbear attacks.  
Its injuries show wolf bites and scorch marks — clues to the wolves’ new abilities.

# Scene 5 — The Wolves’ Den

**Location:** Western woods, cave system with a smoke vent.  
**Outside:**  
- Wolves milling peacefully  
- Tripwires with bone noisemakers  
- Wolves wearing scraps of cloth and simple armor  

**Inside the Cave:**  
- **Central chamber:** sleeping area, crude drawings  
- **West chamber:** stolen goods and livestock  
- **East chamber:** meeting room with fire and smoke vent  
- **Nursery:** wolf pups (use Cat stats)  

---

## Flame & Bolt (Alpha Pair)

- **Flame:** aggressive, fire breath  
- **Bolt:** cautious, lightning breath  
- Both speak Common  
- Both are awakened wolves with enhanced intelligence  

---

## Social Encounter Option

Bolt is willing to negotiate:  
- Wolves gained intelligence after attacking the missing sorcerer  
- They want safety, food security, and territory  
- Flame wants revenge and dominance  

Negotiation triggers:  
- Four wolves die  
- Flame or Bolt below half HP  
- A PC drops to 0 HP  

If negotiations succeed, Flame betrays Bolt, triggering a final fight.

# Scene 6 — Resolution

## If the party kills the wolves:
- They fulfill the contract  
- They may find pups and desperate survivors  
- Council pays full reward  

## If the party negotiates:
- Bolt offers peace or relocation  
- Council must be convinced  
- Tillus opposes negotiations  
- Persuasion checks determine outcome

- If wolves’ intelligence becomes known, Featherock recovers faster

# Key NPC Summaries

## Tillus Merrion
- Halfling leader of the Growers & Buyers Association  
- Proud, orderly, obsessed with trade  
- Fears economic collapse more than hunger  
- Not evil — just rigid and anxious  

## Leanor Slatebeard
- Dwarven innkeeper  
- Warm, practical, supportive of adventurers  
- Runs the Shepherd’s Crook with her husband Banteth  

## Featherock
- Halfling shepherd  
- Traumatized survivor  
- Heard wolves speaking during the ambush
