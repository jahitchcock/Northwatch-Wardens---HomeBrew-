# Temple of the Dragonknights - DM Notes

*Quick reference and guidance for running this adventure*

\page

## Quick Reference

**Recommended Level:** 4-5 (ideal for level 4-5, capstone adventure)  
**Session Length:** 6-8 hours (or 2-3 sessions)  
**Party Size:** 3-4 players (scalable to 2-5)  
**Key NPCs:** Venomfang (young green dragon), dragon cultists, Sera Gelanadel (rescued wizard)  
**Echo Clue:** Dragon cult exploiting the Echo, multiple factions aware, direct confrontation with Echo-amplified magic  
**Primary Location:** Temple of the Dragonknights (northwest mountains)

**Tone:** Epic capstone, combat-heavy, high stakes, Season 1 finale

\page

## The Big Picture

### What's Really Happening

**The Truth:**
- Venomfang, a young green dragon, occupied ancient Dragonknight burial site
- Kobold minions raid nearby settlements for treasure and victims
- Dragon cult of Tiamat is performing rituals in the temple
- They're using Aeorian artifacts to create soul gems
- Goal: revive a Dracolich (or similar undead dragon)
- The Echo is amplifying their dark magic
- Multiple factions now know about the Echo's power
- Joel's daughter (Clementine) was kidnapped for ritual sacrifice
- Temple location is nexus of Echo energy

**The Conspiracy:**
- Cultists deliberately chose this site (Echo-active)
- Venomfang is pawn, not mastermind
- Soul gem ritual uses Aeorian magic + Echo power
- Dracolich revival would be catastrophic
- This is just the beginning of larger draconic plot
- Sets up Season 2 threats

\page

### Echo Connection

**What Players Learn:**
- **CRITICAL REVELATION:** Multiple factions know about Echo
- Dragon cults can weaponize Echo energy
- Echo amplifies necromancy and transformation magic
- Ancient burial sites serve as power nexuses
- Aeorian artifacts + Echo = unprecedented power
- Someone is coordinating exploitation of Echo

**What Elric Will Want:**
- Full details on cult ritual
- Soul gem creation process
- How they're channeling Echo
- Any recovered ritual texts
- Information on who leads the cult
- Evidence of Dracolich connection

**Campaign Significance:**
- **SEASON ONE FINALE REVELATION**
- Echo is not just ambient—it's being exploited
- Multiple groups know and are racing to control it
- Aeorian + Echo combination is extremely dangerous
- Heroes must now protect the region from deliberate attacks
- Sets up Season 2: "Who else knows? What are they planning?"

## Five Major Dungeons/Sections

### Section 1: Andersmith Farm (Hook)

**What Happens:** Scene of tragedy, trail begins

**Purpose:**
- Emotional hook (murdered family)
- Introduce Joel Andersmith (survivor)
- Evidence of kobold raid
- Trail to Poisontip Cavern
- Establish stakes

**Key Locations:**
- Burned farmhouse
- Dead family members
- Ransacked barn
- Blood trail to forest

**Investigation Reveals:**
- Kobolds attacked at night
- Daughter Clementine taken alive
- Parents and son killed
- Supplies stolen
- Direction: north to cavern

\page

### Section 2: Poisontip Cavern (Kobold Lair)

**What Happens:** Fight through kobold-infested caves

**Purpose:**
- Combat encounters
- Resource depletion before temple
- Learn about dragon master
- Recover village supplies
- Find clues about temple location

**Dungeon Features:**
- Natural caves
- Extremely dark (need darkvision/light)
- Rickety bridges over stalagmite pits
- Traps (pressure plates, rock falls)
- Underground lake with quippers
- Two-level structure

**Key Encounters:**
- Area 1: Winged kobold sentinel
- Area 2: Sleeping kobolds in tents
- Area 3: Bridge crossing + trap
- Area 4: Piercer ambush + treasure chest
- Area 5: Puzzle door (blood and fire offering)

\page

### Section 3: Temple Approach (Burial Ground)

**What Happens:** Cross threshold into ancient temple

**Purpose:**
- Transition to epic final dungeon
- Reveal temple architecture
- Show Dragonknight history
- Build tension
- Puzzle door challenge

**The Puzzle:**
- Offering bowl filled with gold
- Door has Dragonknight emblem
- Two magical torches on walls
- Solution: Pour blood into bowl, light with torch
- "Legends are born through blood and fire"

**Lore:**
- Dragonknights were ancient dragon-slayers
- Buried with honor in this temple
- Their emblem: snake-like creature wrapped around sword
- Temple has been desecrated by dragon cult

\page

### Section 4: Temple Interior (Multi-Level)

**What Happens:** Explore desecrated temple, fight undead and cultists

**Purpose:**
- Major combat encounters
- Rescue Sera Gelanadel (wizard NPC)
- Discover cult's plans
- Find Dragonknight artifacts
- Learn about ritual

**Dungeon Features:**
- Two-level stone temple
- Torn and burnt banners
- Coffins of fallen Dragonknights
- Ritual chamber with active magic
- Armory with ancient weapons
- Cultist quarters
- Frieze depicting dragon-slaying history

**Key Encounters:**
- Area 1: Four Dragonknight zombies (step on emblem = trigger)
- Area 2: Armory + tortured wizard (Sera)
- Area 3: Shadow ambush in art gallery
- Area 4: Five cultists performing soul gem ritual on Clementine
- Area 5: Cultist sleeping quarters

\page

### Section 5: Mountaintop Lair (Dragon Battle)

**What Happens:** Final confrontation with Venomfang

**Purpose:**
- Epic boss battle
- Reveal dragon's eggs
- Dragon wyrmling fight
- Capstone for Season 1
- Set up future consequences

**Location:**
- Open mountaintop clearing
- Peaceful forest visible
- Toppled Dragonknight statue
- Four dragon eggs (one hatched)
- Marble platform

**The Fight:**
- Green dragon wyrmling stalks party
- Attacks when they reach eggs
- Can flee to bring mother
- Or fight to death protecting eggs
- Environmental cover (statue, trees)

**If Venomfang Returns:**
- Young green dragon (much more dangerous)
- Finds lair attacked
- One egg missing
- Seeks terrible revenge
- Sets up Season 2 nemesis

## Resolution Paths

### Path 1: Complete Victory

**How:** Rescue Clementine, defeat all enemies, kill wyrmling

**Outcomes:**
- Clementine saved and returned to Joel
- Village supplies recovered
- Cultists defeated (ritual stopped)
- Temple purified
- Dragon egg(s) destroyed or taken
- Venomfang returns to find ruin
- 450 XP from wyrmling + 125 XP from cultists + other encounters

**Rewards:**
- 100 gp from village supplies return
- Joel's eternal gratitude
- Village honors heroes
- Elric fascinated by soul gem ritual info
- Sera becomes ally
- +1 Spear of the Dragonknights
- Platinum ring (250 gp)
- Multiple healing potions
- Reputation as dragon-slayers

\page

### Path 2: Partial Success (Clementine Dies)

**How:** Arrive too late, ritual completes

**Outcomes:**
- Clementine's soul trapped in gem
- Cultists use it to begin dracolich summoning
- Harder final fight (powered-up cultists)
- Joel devastated
- Bittersweet victory
- Soul gem might be recoverable (Season 2 hook)

**Consequences:**
- Party feels guilt
- Village mourns
- Reduced rewards
- Darker tone
- "You stopped the ritual but couldn't save her"

\page

### Path 3: Negotiate with Venomfang

**How:** Don't fight wyrmling, make deal

**Extremely Difficult:**
- Wyrmling is young, aggressive
- Protective of siblings
- Doesn't speak Common well
- Mother taught it to fear/hate humanoids

**If Successful:**
- Promise to kill cultists
- Leave eggs alone
- Wyrmling lets party pass
- Or even helps against cultists
- Venomfang less vengeful

**Outcomes:**
- Avoid wyrmling fight
- Dragon family survives
- Green dragons as neutral force
- Complex future relationship
- Creative solution rewarded

\page

### Path 4: Take Dragon Egg

**Dark Choice:**
- Steal one or more eggs
- Use as leverage or sell
- Raise dragon (very difficult)
- Venomfang's eternal wrath

**Consequences:**
- Venomfang hunts party forever
- Young green dragon is CR 8 (deadly)
- Becomes recurring villain
- Moral weight
- Egg hatches eventually (problem)

**If Raise Dragon:**
- Requires expertise
- Sera could help
- Years of effort
- Green dragons are evil (difficult)
- But infant could be influenced?
- Long-term campaign arc

## Running the Adventure

### Pacing and Structure

**This is a MARATHON adventure:**
- Multiple dungeons back-to-back
- Resource attrition is key
- No long rests between sections
- Build exhaustion and tension
- Climax at dragon battle

**Recommended Breaks:**
- After Poisontip Cavern (short rest)
- After Temple entrance puzzle (breath)
- After rescuing Sera (short rest)
- After stopping ritual (short rest if needed)
- Final push to mountaintop

**Total Combat Encounters:** 8-12 depending on choices
**Resource Management:** Players should feel challenged by finale

\page

### The Moral Complexity

**No Clear Villains:**
- Venomfang is protective mother
- Cultists believe they serve greater good
- Kobolds are just following master
- Joel wants revenge (understandable)
- Clementine is innocent victim

**Questions to Raise:**
- Is killing dragon eggs justified?
- Are cultists evil or misguided?
- What happens to surviving kobolds?
- Does Joel get closure?
- What about dragon's revenge?

**Multiple Perspectives:**
- Heroes' duty to stop cult
- Dragon's right to protect young
- Village's need for safety
- Cultists' fanatical devotion
- Innocent lives at stake

\page

### Joel Andersmith's Role

**Background:**
- Farmer from Andersmith Farm
- Wife and son murdered
- Daughter kidnapped
- Witnessed kobold attack
- Traumatized but determined

**Personality:**
- Normally gentle, kind man
- Currently consumed by grief and rage
- Wants revenge on those who took his family
- Desperate to save Clementine
- Will risk everything

**Key Lines:**
- "They killed my wife. My son. They took my little girl."
- "Please, you have to save Clementine. She's all I have left."
- "I don't care about treasure or glory. Just bring her home."
- "If she's dead... make them pay."

**Resolution:**
- If Clementine saved: "You gave me back my reason to live."
- If Clementine dies: Hollow shell, broken man, might seek death
- Needs party's support either way

\page

### Sera Gelanadel's Role

**Background:**
- Elven wizard, level 3-4
- Sent to investigate arcane anomaly
- Captured by cultists
- Tortured for information
- Knows about Echo

**Personality:**
- Intelligent and scholarly
- Brave despite torture
- Grateful to rescuers
- Wants to understand Echo
- Connects with Elric's research

**Key Lines:**
- "Thank you. I thought I would die here."
- "The cultists spoke of ancient power. Something called 'The Echo.'"
- "They're amplifying Aeorian magic somehow. It's unprecedented."
- "I must report this to my order. And to your Lorewarden Elric."

**Combat Role:**
- Wizard abilities (Mage stat block)
- Has 2 healing potions
- Can support party in fights
- Exhausted but determined

**Future:**
- Returns to her wizard college
- Shares findings with Elric
- Becomes recurring ally
- Might join for Season 2

## Common Player Approaches

### "We want to bring Joel with us"

**Complicated:**

**Arguments For:**
- It's his daughter
- He deserves to help
- Extra fighter

**Arguments Against:**
- Not a combatant
- Will die easily
- Emotional decisions
- Might compromise mission

**How to Handle:**
- Joel insists on coming
- Give him commoner stats (4 HP)
- He stays back in fights
- Rushes forward if sees Clementine
- Needs protection
- Emotional burden on party

**Alternative:**
- Convince him to stay (DC 15 Persuasion)
- "We'll bring her back. Trust us."
- He waits at village, praying

\page

### "We want to avoid all the kobolds"

**Stealth Approach:**
- Possible in Poisontip Cavern
- Sleeping kobolds can be bypassed
- Traps still trigger
- Puzzle door still required

**How to Run:**
- Group stealth checks
- Noise attracts attention
- Some encounters unavoidable (winged kobold, puzzle guardian)
- Reward cleverness with XP
- But miss treasure from tents

**Outcomes:**
- Conserve resources
- Reach temple faster
- Kobolds might follow later
- Or attack during dragon fight

\page

### "We want to convert the cultists"

**Difficult:**
- Cultists are fanatics
- Worship Tiamat
- Believe in their cause
- Won't betray goddess

**If Attempted:**
- DC 20 Persuasion or Religion check
- Requires compelling theological argument
- Might convert 1-2 at most
- Others attack anyway
- Converted cultists provide information
- Or sabotage ritual from inside

**Better Approach:**
- Intimidate into fleeing (DC 15)
- Offer escape if they release Clementine
- Turn them against each other
- Disrupt ritual before talking

\page

### "We want to ally with Venomfang"

**Evil Dragon:**
- Young green dragon
- Chaotic evil alignment
- Manipulative and deceptive
- Sees humanoids as tools or food

**If Attempted:**
- Dragon might pretend to ally
- Plans to betray party
- Or genuinely cooperate temporarily
- Against shared enemy (cultists)
- Then turns on party after

**Requirements:**
- Extremely high Deception or Persuasion
- Offer something valuable
- Convince dragon party is useful
- Don't threaten eggs

**Outcomes:**
- Temporary alliance against cult
- Dragon "forgets" deal afterward
- Or honors it barely
- Sets up complex relationship

\page

### "We want to rescue Sera but skip the ritual"

**Time Pressure:**
- Ritual is ongoing in Area 4
- Every delay = progress toward completion
- If take too long, Clementine dies

**Tracking Time:**
- Cavern exploration: 1 hour
- Temple lower level: 30 minutes
- Rescuing Sera: 10 minutes
- Travel between: 15 minutes
- **Ritual completes in 2 hours from farm**

**Rush Option:**
- Skip optional areas
- Run through encounters
- Make strategic choices
- Arrive before completion

**Or:**
- Take time to explore
- Rescue Sera first
- Prepare thoroughly
- Arrive too late
- Harder fight but party stronger

## The Soul Gem Ritual

### What's Happening

**The Ritual:**
- Cultists channel Echo + Aeorian magic
- Extract victim's soul
- Trap in magical gem
- Soul gem used to create phylactery
- Phylactery binds dragon's spirit
- Creates Dracolich

**Current Status:**
- In progress when party arrives
- Clementine suspended by magic beams
- Five cultists channeling power
- Runic circle glowing
- 60-75% complete depending on party speed

**If Completed:**
- Clementine's soul ripped out
- Body drops dead
- Soul gem forms (glowing crystal)
- Cultists attempt to escape with it
- Harder fight (soul-powered)
- Dark victory if gem recovered

\page

### How to Stop It

**Interrupt the Ritual:**
- Attack cultists (breaks concentration)
- Disrupt runic circle (Arcana DC 15)
- Counter-spell (if party has it)
- Destroy magical focus
- Kill lead cultist

**Consequences of Interruption:**
- Magical backlash (all in room take 2d6 force damage)
- Clementine falls unconscious
- Cultists attack immediately
- Soul gem doesn't form
- Clementine survives but traumatized

**If Too Late:**
- Ritual completes before party arrives
- Soul gem formed
- Clementine's body dead
- Cultists empowered (temp HP, advantage)
- Darker tone for rest of adventure

\page

### Investigation Reveals

**Podium Book (DC 15 Arcana):**
- Soul extraction ritual
- Requires Aeorian artifact + Echo resonance
- Victim must be innocent (pure soul)
- Creates perfect phylactery material

**DC 14 History Check:**
- Soul gems used in lich creation
- Dracolich requires dragon's willing participation
- Or soul gem + resurrection magic
- Incredibly dangerous

**Elric's Future Analysis:**
- "They're combining Aeorian necromancy with Echo amplification."
- "This proves multiple factions know about the Echo."
- "Dracolich creation... unprecedented in modern times."
- "We must find who leads this cult."

## Complications & Twists

### If Venomfang Arrives During Temple Fight

**Dramatic Entrance:**
- Temple roof explodes
- Green dragon lands amid battle
- "WHO DARES INVADE MY DOMAIN?"
- Attacks everyone (cultists too)

**How to Handle:**
- Party + cultists vs. dragon (temporary alliance)
- Or party flees while dragon kills cultists
- Or negotiate with dragon (very difficult)
- Epic chaos

**Outcomes:**
- Higher casualty rate
- Clementine might die in crossfire
- Dragon might spare party if they help
- Or remember them for future revenge

\page

### If Party Takes Too Long

**Time Consequences:**

**2+ hours since farm:**
- Ritual completes
- Clementine dies
- Soul gem created
- Cultists empowered

**3+ hours:**
- Venomfang returns
- Finds temple invaded
- Attacks everyone

**4+ hours:**
- Cultists escaped with soul gem
- Temple abandoned
- Trail leads elsewhere
- Hook for Season 2

**Track Time Carefully:**
- Each encounter = 10-20 minutes
- Exploration = 5-10 minutes per area
- Rests = 1 hour (short) or 8 hours (long)
- Build pressure

\page

### If Sera Dies

**Loss of Ally:**
- Party loses wizard support
- Sera's knowledge lost
- Her wizard order upset
- No future collaboration

**Who Investigates Then:**
- Elric works alone
- Or party must fill gap
- Harder to understand Echo
- Lose potential Season 2 ally

**Prevent This:**
- Sera has 2 healing potions
- Can disengage and hide
- Cultists focused on ritual, not her
- Make saving her feel heroic

\page

### If All Eggs Destroyed

**Venomfang's Wrath:**
- Loses entire clutch
- Inconsolable rage
- Seeks party forever
- Will follow to ends of earth
- No negotiation possible

**Regional Consequences:**
- Green dragon terrorizes Northreach
- Attacks villages looking for party
- Becomes regional threat
- Requires dragon-slaying quest
- Escalates to war

**Better:**
- Leave at least one egg
- Dragon has reason to avoid war
- Focused on protecting survivor
- Allows future plots

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Don't:**
- Rush the investigation at farm
- Let time pressure vanish
- Make ritual inevitable (give chance to stop)
- Kill Joel or Sera casually
- Bring Venomfang in unfairly
- Forget emotional weight (dead family)
- Skip Echo revelation (crucial!)
- End Season 1 without setup for 2
- Make kobolds too easy (resource drain important)
- Forget environmental hazards

**Do:**
- Play up tragedy of Andersmith family
- Track time carefully
- Make ritual interruption feel epic
- Show cultists' fanaticism
- Describe dragon magnificently
- Connect all Echo clues
- Give proper Season 1 ending
- Set up Season 2 hooks
- Reward heroism and cleverness
- Make it feel like capstone adventure

## Troubleshooting

### "Party is too beat up for dragon fight"

**Options:**
- Sera shares her healing potions
- Find extra potions in cultist areas
- Allow short rest before mountaintop
- Reduce wyrmling HP
- Or let them feel challenged (not unfair)

### "Clementine died in ritual"

**Dark Outcome:**
- Joel broken
- Village mourns
- Party feels weight
- Soul gem becomes quest item
- "Can we resurrect her in Season 2?"
- Sets up redemption arc

### "They want to fight Venomfang"

**Young Green Dragon CR 8:**
- Will likely TPK level 4-5 party
- But if they insist...
- Make it clear it's deadly
- "She is ENORMOUS. Her breath could melt stone."
- Survival check: "You will die."
- Allow retreat/negotiation

### "Running two dungeons is too much"

**Options:**
- Split across multiple sessions
- Allow long rest between (reduces tension though)
- Skip Poisontip Cavern (go straight to temple)
- But lose setup and resource drain
- Or simplify/streamline kobold lair

### "Party wants to keep soul gem"

**Dangerous:**
- Contains Clementine's soul
- Cultist phylactery material
- Radiates evil necromancy
- Corrupts bearer over time
- Elric needs it for research

**Compromise:**
- Elric studies it safely
- Attempts to free Clementine's soul
- Requires high-level magic (Season 2)
- Party can help
