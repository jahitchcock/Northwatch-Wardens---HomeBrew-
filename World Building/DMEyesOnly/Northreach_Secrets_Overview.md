## The Big Secret: The Aeorian Echo

**What It Is:**
Magical energy emanating from Salsvault (buried Aeorian ruins) in Northreach's far north.

**What It Does:**
- Awakens intelligence in animals
- Spreads Aeorian diseases (frigid woe)
- Activates ancient artifacts
- Destabilizes magic
- Attracts power-seekers

**Season 1 Scope:**
Players learn about Echo, understand its effects, confront those exploiting it. Actually dealing with Salsvault is Season 2+.

## Revelation Guidelines

### Don't Reveal Everything

**Players Should Discover:**
- Through investigation
- By asking NPCs
- During adventures
- Via consequences

**Not Through:**
- DM exposition dumps
- Reading secrets aloud
- Unprompted revelations

\page

### Layer Information

**Surface Level (Free Information):**
- Northreach is frontier region
- Guild protects settlements
- Strange things happening lately

**Investigation Level (Active Seeking):**
- Echo causing magical anomalies
- Aeorian ruins in far north
- Factions taking interest

**Deep Secrets (Earned Discovery):**
- NPC hidden agendas
- Salsvault's true nature
- Long-term threats

\page

## Season 1 vs. Long-Term Secrets

### Season 1 Scope
**Should Be Revealed:**
- Echo exists and its general effects
- Salsvault is the source
- Factions are exploiting it
- Northreach is in danger

**Can Remain Secret:**
- How to stop the Echo
- Salsvault's full history
- Other Aeorian sites
- Larger world implications

### Season 2+ Scope
**Save For Later:**
- Entering Salsvault
- Confronting Echo directly
- Other Aeorian mysteries
- Expanding to wider world

\page

## Quick Reference: What Players Should Know By When

**After Open Skirmish:**
- Something's wrong with wildlife
- Elric is investigating

**After First Adventure:**
- Multiple locations affected
- Pattern emerging

**Mid-Campaign (3-4 adventures):**
- "Aeorian Echo" named
- Ancient magic involved
- Getting worse over time

**Late Campaign (5+ adventures):**
- Salsvault is source
- Far north location
- Factions exploiting
- Must take action

**After Temple:**
- Full picture clear
- Season 1 threats resolved
- Season 2 hints
- Ongoing mystery

\page

## Remember

**These secrets enhance the campaign but aren't mandatory.**

Players can enjoy adventures without knowing every detail. Treat secrets as:
- Tools for deepening story
- Rewards for investigation
- Connections between adventures
- DM reference, not script

**Let players discover at their own pace. Mystery is more engaging than exposition.**
