## When to Use These

### Season 1 (Northreach Campaign)

**Minimal Use:**
- Player backstories from other regions
- Trading connections
- NPC origins
- World-building flavor

**Don't Emphasize:** Focus stays on Northreach

\page

### Season 2+

**When Players:**
- Complete Northreach arc
- Want to explore wider world
- Pursue faction connections
- Seek greater threats

**These regions become:** Adventure locations, faction homes, plot hooks

## Quick Reference

**For Season 1:**

## Regional Themes

**Nullwood:** Dark forest, ancient magic  
**Stonebound Depths:** Underground, dwarven ruins  
**Vharoxis:** Desert, dragon lairs  
**Shattered Coast:** Pirates, naval adventure  
**Emberlands:** Volcanic, fire giants  
**Verdant Marches:** Swamp, yuan-ti  
**Sunken Dominion:** Underwater, aboleth

\page

## Integration Tips

### Player Backstories

**If player from another region:**
- Read that region's secrets
- Work with player on ties
- Create connection to Northreach

### NPC Origins

**Use other regions for:**
- Traveling merchants
- Guild members' backgrounds
- Villain origins
- Mysterious strangers

### Rumors and News

**Occasional mentions:**
- "Trader from Emberlands says..."
- "News from Shattered Coast..."
- "Mara's network reports..."

**Keep it background:** Don't distract from Northreach

\page

## Final Note

**Season 1 is intentionally focused.**

Northreach provides rich, self-contained campaign. These broader world secrets are **future tools**, not Season 1 requirements.

**Use them when:**
- Players ask about wider world
- You need flavor details
- Planning future seasons
- Player backstories require it

**Until then:** Focus on Northreach, the Aeorian Echo, and Season 1 adventures.
