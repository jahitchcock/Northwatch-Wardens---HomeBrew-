# Northreach 

The Frontier Region

::**Theme:** Frontier survival
**Tone:** Gritty, isolated, dangerous
Northreach is a cold, sparsely settled frontier region most inhabitants know only local concerns—wolves, bandits, missing hunters.
This is the home of the Northwatch Wardens.


## Geography

### Regional Characteristics
- **Climate:** Cold temperate to subarctic
- **Terrain:** Rolling hills, dense forests, coastal plains, mountains to the north
- **Water:** Rivers, coastal access to the northeast
- **Settlement Pattern:** Scattered villages, isolated farmsteads, few towns
- **Infrastructure:** Rough roads, limited trade routes, frontier conditions


Northreach's geography follows a practical travel network centered on **Waystone Inn**, with all major locations reachable through established routes.

## Culture and Society

### Frontier Mentality
- **Self-reliance:** Communities depend on themselves first
- **Mutual aid:** Neighbors help neighbors survive
- **Practical pragmatism:** Survival trumps ideology
- **Wary of strangers:** Trust must be earned
- **Respect for competence:** Skill and reliability matter more than background


### Social Structure
- **Village councils:** Local governance by respected community members
- **Guilds:** Trade organizations provide structure (Growers, Fleecers, etc.)
- **Religious leaders:** Priests offer guidance but limited authority
- **Adventurers:** Wardens and travelers treated with cautious respect

\page

### Daily Life
- **Agriculture:** Wheat, sheep, limited crops in short growing season
- **Trade:** Essential goods flow slowly through rough roads
- **Communication:** News travels by travelers; weeks between updates
- **Magic:** Rare, mistrusted, but increasingly necessary
- **Threats:** Wolves, bandits, weather

## Factions and Organizations

### The Northwatch Wardens (Player Organization)
**Type:** Guild/mercenary company  
**Purpose:** Protect frontier settlements through contract work  
**Size:** ~20-30 active members (variable)  
**Structure:** Loose, drop-in friendly, mission-based

**Leadership Philosophy:**
- Marshal Thorne: "Solve problems, protect people"
- Steward Fenwick: "Stay equipped, stay fed, stay alive"
- Lorewarden Vael: "Understand what we're fighting"

**Services Provided:**
- Monster elimination
- Investigation
- Escort and protection
- Artifact recovery
- Dispute resolution

### Local Governance
**Village Councils:**
- Elected or respected elders
- Make local policy
- Manage resources
- Hire Wardens for problems beyond their capability

**Guilds:**
- Growers' Association (farmers)
- Fleecers' Guild (shepherds)
- Tradespeople (limited specialization)
