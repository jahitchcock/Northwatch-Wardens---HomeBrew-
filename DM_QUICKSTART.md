## Step 5: Understand Player Choices Matter

**Key Decision in Wolves of Welton:**
- **Kill the wolves** â€” Quick solution, but misses the mystery

**Your Role:** Present all options, let players choose, then show consequences.

**No "right" answer** â€” different choices lead to different outcomes.

## Common First-Time DM Questions

**Q: What if players refuse the contract?**  
**A:** Brenna says, "That's fine. There are other contracts." Show them 2â€“3 other options (point to different adventures).

**Q: What if I mess up the rules?**  
**A:** That's fine! Make a ruling, keep the game moving, look it up later. Players care more about story than perfect mechanics.

**Q: What if players kill an important NPC?**  
**A:** Let it happen. Actions have consequences. Someone else steps up, or the guild changes. The campaign is flexible.

**Q: Do I need to prep all adventures?**  
**A:** No! Only prep the one you'll run next. Adventures are modular â€” prep as you go.

**Q: What level should characters start?**  
**A:** Level 1 or 2 is perfect. Use pre-made characters from the [Premade PCs folder](./Premade%20PCs/) if needed.
